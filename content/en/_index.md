1.  **Quwan and Peking University** have unveiled an emotional trajectory evaluation framework! ü§Ø The [ETrajEval framework](https://www.jiqizhixin.com/articles/2025-12-07-3) (AI News) simulates long-term conversations using Markov processes, dynamically checking a model's emotional support capabilities. 2.  **Cornell** has proposed a GAN-like training method called PasoDoble! üí° This framework uses adversarial training with two models: a Proposer that generates tough problems and gets 'difficulty rewards,' and a Solver that solves them and receives 'correctness feedback.'
